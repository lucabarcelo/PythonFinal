So what exactly is happening here?

Since I couldn't unhash the hash you provided, I had to use a dictionary scan

A dictionary scan takes a preallocated list of x number of possible word/number combos and hashes each word in the list...

This allows me to compare the hash you provided me with x number of other hashes I randomly put into this list.

If I couldn't guess your hash, then you have revealed my weakness...

Essentially I can only crack about 500k hashes right now, since the list I run on top of only has 500,000 words to compare from!

So the more basic the word/number combo you provide, the greater the chance it's in my list, and the easier it is for me to crack!
